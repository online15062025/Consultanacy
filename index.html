How we work for your job

  • 1

    Shortlisting

    The consultancy reviews the companys job requirements and carefully shortlists candidates whose skills, experience, and background match the role.

  • 2

    Offer

    After selection, the employer issues a job offer outlining salary, role responsibilities, and employment terms to the chosen candidate.

  • 3

    Visa

    The selected candidate begins the visa application process with required documents and guidance provided by the consultancy.

  • 4

    Emigration

    Once the visa is approved, the candidate completes emigration formalities and prepares for relocation to the destination country.

  • 5

    Travel

    The candidate travels to the destination country and reports to the employer as per the joining schedule.
